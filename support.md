# Support

## Contact Us

For any questions, issues, or feedback about Mindful Dailies, please contact us:

- **Email:** mindful.dailies@gmail.com

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account in the app. Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

### When do puzzles reset?

All puzzles reset at midnight UTC daily.

### I found a bug. How do I report it?

Email us at mindful.dailies@gmail.com with a description of the issue, your device model, and Android version.

### How do I manage notifications?

Go to Settings > Notifications in the app to customize your notification preferences.
