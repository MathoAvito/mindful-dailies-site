# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 6, 2026  
**Last Updated:** April 6, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to Mindful Dailies ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect when you use the Mindful Dailies mobile application (the "App"), how we use that data, and your rights regarding your information.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the email address above.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

When you sign in to the App, we collect:

- **Email Address:** Obtained through Google or Apple sign-in authentication
- **Display Name:** Your name associated with your Google or Apple account
- **User ID:** A unique identifier assigned by AWS Cognito authentication service

### 1.2 App Activity Data

To provide gameplay features and track your progress, we collect:

- **Game Scores:** Your performance on daily puzzles (time to complete, accuracy, number of attempts)
- **Puzzle Completion Data:** Which puzzles you've completed and when
- **Streak Data:** Your current and longest consecutive play streaks
- **Gameplay Statistics:** Win/loss records, average completion times, and performance metrics
- **Friends List:** Users you've added as friends within the App
- **Leaderboard Data:** Your rankings compared to other users

### 1.3 Device Information

We collect limited device information:

- **Push Notification Token:** Generated by Expo Notifications to send you daily reminders and streak warnings
- **Device Type:** iOS or Android, for compatibility purposes
- **App Version:** To ensure you're using a compatible version

### 1.4 App Performance Data

- **Crash Logs:** If the app crashes, anonymous crash reports may be collected to help us improve stability
- **Analytics Events:** Usage patterns to understand which features are most popular (via Firebase Analytics)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### 2.1 Essential App Functions

- **Authentication:** To verify your identity and provide secure access to your account
- **Game Progress:** To save your puzzle completions, scores, and streaks
- **Social Features:** To enable friend connections, leaderboards, and the nudge mechanic
- **Notifications:** To send daily reminders, streak warnings, and friend nudges (if you've enabled them)

### 2.2 App Improvement

- **Analytics:** To understand how users interact with the App and identify areas for improvement
- **Bug Fixes:** To diagnose and resolve technical issues
- **Feature Development:** To prioritize new features based on usage patterns

### 2.3 Communication

- **Service Updates:** To notify you about significant changes to the App or this Privacy Policy
- **Support Responses:** To respond to your inquiries or support requests

We will **never** use your data for advertising, marketing to third parties, or selling your information.

## 3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We use third-party services to operate the App. These services may have access to your data to perform tasks on our behalf:

### 3.1 Third-Party Services

- **AWS Cognito:** Authentication and user identity management
- **Amazon Web Services (AWS):** Cloud hosting for our backend (Lambda, RDS PostgreSQL, Redis ElastiCache)
- **Firebase Analytics (Google):** Anonymous usage analytics to improve the App
- **Expo Notifications (Expo.dev):** Push notification delivery infrastructure
- **Apple/Google Sign-In:** Authentication providers (they receive only the fact that you're signing into our app)

### 3.2 Data Sharing Policies

- We **do not sell** your personal information to third parties
- We **do not share** your data with advertisers
- We **only share** data with the service providers listed above, and only as necessary to operate the App
- These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the specified purposes

### 3.3 Legal Compliance

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as:

- In response to a valid court order or subpoena
- To comply with legal processes
- To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users
- In connection with a business transfer (merger, acquisition, or sale of assets)

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored in the following locations:

- **Cloud Storage:** AWS servers located in [region - to be specified]
- **Local Device Storage:** Game progress and preferences are cached on your device using MMKV encrypted storage
- **Duration:** Data is retained as long as your account is active, plus 30 days after account deletion (for backup purposes)

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

- **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted between the App and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **Encryption at Rest:** Database storage (RDS PostgreSQL) is encrypted, and local device storage uses MMKV encryption
- **Authentication Tokens:** AWS Cognito manages secure session tokens with automatic expiration
- **Access Controls:** Backend systems are protected with strict access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Data Access

You can access your personal information at any time by:

- Viewing your profile in the Settings screen
- Viewing your game history and statistics within the App
- Contacting us at privacy@mindful-dailies.com to request a copy of your data

### 5.2 Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data:

- **In-App Deletion:** Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
- **Email Request:** Contact privacy@mindful-dailies.com with your email address
- **Deletion Timeline:** Account data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request

Once deleted, the following data is removed:

- Your email, display name, and user ID
- All game scores, puzzle completions, and streaks
- Friend connections and leaderboard entries
- Push notification tokens

**Note:** Aggregate, anonymized analytics data may be retained for statistical purposes, but it cannot be linked back to you.

### 5.3 Notification Preferences

You can control notifications at any time:

- **In-App:** Settings > Notifications > Toggle individual notification types
- **Device Level:** iOS Settings > Mindful Dailies > Notifications / Android Settings > Apps > Mindful Dailies > Notifications

### 5.4 Data Export (Portability)

If you wish to export your data to another service:

- Contact privacy@mindful-dailies.com with a data export request
- We will provide your data in JSON format within 14 days

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is **not intended for users under 13 years of age** (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If we discover that we have collected data from a child without parental consent, we will delete that information immediately. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@mindful-dailies.com.

## 7. International Data Transfers

The App is hosted on AWS servers. Depending on your location, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States.

By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your data to these locations. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## 8. Compliance with Privacy Laws

### 8.1 GDPR (European Union)

If you are located in the EU, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Right to Erasure:** Delete your account and data (see Section 5.2)
- **Right to Restriction:** Limit how we process your data
- **Right to Data Portability:** Receive your data in a portable format
- **Right to Object:** Object to certain types of data processing
- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent for notifications or data collection (by deleting your account)

**Legal Basis for Processing:**

- **Contractual Necessity:** To provide the App services you've requested
- **Legitimate Interests:** To improve the App and ensure security
- **Consent:** For optional features like notifications

**Data Protection Officer:** privacy@mindful-dailies.com

### 8.2 CCPA (California, USA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

- **Right to Know:** What personal information we collect and how it's used
- **Right to Delete:** Request deletion of your personal information (see Section 5.2)
- **Right to Opt-Out:** We do not sell your personal information, so no opt-out is necessary
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** You will not receive discriminatory treatment for exercising your rights

To exercise your rights, contact privacy@mindful-dailies.com.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes:

- The "Last Updated" date at the top will be revised
- We will notify you via in-app notification or email if the changes are significant
- Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

## 10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The App does not use cookies. However, we do use:

- **Firebase Analytics SDK:** Collects anonymous usage data (device type, app version, session duration, screen views)
- **Local Storage (MMKV):** Stores game progress and preferences on your device

You cannot disable analytics without uninstalling the App, but all analytics data is anonymous and not linked to your identity.

## 11. Do Not Track Signals

The App does not respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals because it is a native mobile app, not a website. However, you can control data collection by:

- Not signing in (limited features available)
- Deleting your account (see Section 5.2)
- Uninstalling the App

## 12. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to external websites or services (e.g., tutorials, support pages). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. Please review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

## 13. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active, plus:

- **30 days after account deletion:** To allow for recovery if the deletion was accidental
- **Anonymized analytics:** Retained indefinitely for statistical purposes, with no link to your identity
